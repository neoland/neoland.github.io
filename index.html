Our favorite general education websites:


Khanacademy

Personally, I have used Khanacademy for almost four years now.
I have used it to learn things ranging from polynomials to cell
structure to animation. A unique aspect of Khanacademy is that
the website is not categorized into courses, but is more of a drop-in
whenever you want to learn sort of thing. Information on Khanacademy
is easily accessible and the rewards system is enjoyable. Khanacademy
offers a vast variety of topics that are not shoved into specific
courses but offered comprehensively. Khanacademy's math resources cover
everything; from grade school to calculus to recreational math. There
also are great science resources, with chemistry, physics, biology, etc.
Khanacademy has also more recently made AP courses for many subjects.
SAT and other standarized test practice are available, as well as many
niche topics and fun, interesting videos.

Our favorite language learning websites:

Duolingo

I am a long time user of Duolingo. As the golden standard of language
learning, Duolingo now offers courses in Spanish, French, Italian, Chinese,
English, and 28 other languages. Duolingo even offers High Valyrian and
Klingon! Time for the GOT fans to roll in. Duolingo can be found on mobile
as well as on a browser. Duolingo is focused on practice and naturally introduces
you to the grammar and vocabulary of a language. Like all language learning
sites, after completing a language, you may not be able to speak fluently right
after, but Duolingo gets you ready to become a fluent speaker after you finish
their language course. The entire system is cumulative and is built on practice and
review.

Duolingo stands out from the crowd as a truly fast and easy way to learn a language.
It's addictive and fun to use, with features such as Leaderboards (which gives you
a good dose of competition) and Stories (which are stories in the language you are
learning that you can follow along with). It is streamlined and elegant and by far
the most popular way to learn a language online.
