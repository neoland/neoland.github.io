Our favorite general education websites:

Khanacademy

Personally, I have used Khanacademy for almost four years now.
I have used it to learn things ranging from polynomials to cell
structure to animation. A unique aspect of Khanacademy is that
the website is not categorized into courses, but is more of a drop-in
whenever you want to learn sort of thing. Information on Khanacademy
is easily accessible and the rewards system is enjoyable. Khanacademy
offers a vast variety of topics that are not shoved into specific
courses but offered comprehensively. Khanacademy's math resources cover
everything; from grade school to calculus to recreational math. There
also are great science resources, with chemistry, physics, biology, etc.
Khanacademy has also more recently made AP courses for many subjects.
SAT and other standarized test practice are available, as well as many
niche topics and fun, interesting videos.
